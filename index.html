Ingredients

  • cooked rice - 2 cups
  • curd - 2cups
  • edible oil - 1 tsp
  • mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • uraf dal - 1 tsp
  • curry leaves - 6 pc
  • green chilli - 2 pc
  • salt - to taste
  • hing - a pinch
  • dry redvchilli - 1 pc

Procedure

  1. combine the rice and curd together in a bowl.
  2. take a pan put oil in it and then add mustard seeds,urdal dal,hing,dry red chili,hreen chili,and curry leaves and saute it.
  3. now add this mixture to the curd rice prepared.
  4. mix it well and then add salt to it. your detectable south indian curd ready to serve.
